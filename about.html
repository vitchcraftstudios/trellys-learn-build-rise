The Mind Behind Trellys Rise

Mr. Atul Kale | Lead Electrical Engineer

Mr. Atul Kale started Trellys with a clear mission: to empower the next generation of engineers through practical, career-focused learning. Drawing from years of industry experience, he believes that true understanding comes not just from theory, but from hands-on application.

With Trellys, he aims to bridge the gap between classroom concepts and real-world engineering, giving students the confidence, skills, and mindset they need to rise in their careers.

He envisions an environment where students are not only taught theory but also trained to apply it with confidence - from working with live circuits to solving real engineering problems.
