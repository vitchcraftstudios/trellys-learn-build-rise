Learning Programs

Build Skills That Matter

Get hands-on with electrical training. Trellys Rise offers flexible, career-focused courses to help you succeed in the field.

01
Focus Area

Electrical Measuring Instruments

What You'll Learn:

  • Introduction to essential electrical tools
  • Understanding analog & digital measuring instruments
  • Safe handling and practical use in real circuits
  • Interpreting readings with accuracy
  • Building confidence in diagnostic and testing skills
02
Focus Area

Digital Panel Meters & Process Controllers

What You'll Learn:

  • Basics and functions of digital panel meters (DPMs)
  • Applications in electrical and industrial systems
  • Display configuration and parameter settings
  • Introduction to process controllers and control loops
  • Step-by-step programming techniques
  • Real-life integration and troubleshooting examples
03
Focus Area

Sensors & Sensor Controllers

What You'll Learn:

  • Fundamentals of sensors and their classifications
  • Working principles of proximity, photoelectric, temp, and pressure sensors
  • Sensor selection based on application
  • Introduction to sensor controllers and their role in automation
  • Hands-on practice with wiring, calibration, and signal interpretation
  • Real-world industrial use cases
04
Focus Area

Industry-ready Sub-station Design Training

What You'll Learn:

  • Learn the layout and design principles of 11 KV switch yards and substations
  • Understand equipment selection: transformers, isolators, circuit breakers, CT/PTs & busbars
  • Explore protection schemes & earthing systems for safe and reliable operation
  • Gain knowledge of load calculations & system planning for industrial applications
  • Develop practical design skills with real-world case studies and projects
