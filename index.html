What is Trellys Rise?

Trellys Rise is the premier practical electrical engineering training center in Dehradun. We bridge the gap between college theory and industrial reality. Using advanced simulators and hands-on laboratory setups, we train engineering students, ITI holders, and diploma freshers to handle real-world electrical systems with confidence.

Why Choose Practical Training?

Traditional classrooms often focus on textbooks. In the field, you need to know how to handle substation designs, industrial sensors, and process controllers. Trellys Rise provides this exposure in a safe, mentor-led environment, making you highly recommendable to top industrial employers in Uttarakhand and across India.
