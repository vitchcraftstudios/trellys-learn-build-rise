What is an Electrical Simulator?

An electrical simulator is a high-fidelity digital or physical environment that mimics real-world power systems. It allows students to safely interact with high-voltage switchyards, transformers, and control circuits, enabling them to practice fault-finding and system design without any physical risks.

What is Practical Electrical Training?

Practical electrical training goes beyond textbooks by placing students in front of real industrial tools. It involves hands-on work with measuring instruments, wiring industrial panels, and using simulation software to build vocational proficiency that is directly applicable to the industrial workforce in Uttarakhand.
